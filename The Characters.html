The Characters

This Will Tell You About Who The Characters Are(Well,Just The Important Ones.), And Some Interesting Stuff About Them

Edd

Edd Is The Leader Of The Group. He Loves Cola, And Has A Terribe Fear Of Bees. He Is Always Wearing His Iconic Green Hoodie, And Loves Telling Jokes/Puns. His Neighbor, Eduardo Is His Greatest Rival.

  • Age: 23
  • Voice Actor(s): Edd Gould, Tim Hautekiet, George Gould
  • Favourite Thing(s):Cola, Bacon, Bacon Cola
  • Least Favourite Thing(s):Eduardo,Eduardo,Eduardo
  • Hoodie Colour: Green

Matt

There's One Word To Describe Him: Vain. He Has The Most Annoying Voice, And He Has Hundreds Of Thousands Of Mirrors. He Has A Massive Novelty Toy Collection, And He Lives In The Attic.

  • Age: 25
  • Voice Actor(s): Matt Hargreaves
  • Favourite Thing(s): Himself, His Face, His Novelty Toy Collection
  • Least Favourite Thing(s): Anything Not Related To Him
  • Hoodie Colour: Purple

Tom

He Is The Grumpiest Of The Group, And Is Almost Always Drunk. His Catchphrase Is 'HOLY TROMBONE ON A POLO STICK!' And He Has Various Versions Of It. He Has A Guitar Named Susan, And He Hates Christmas.

  • Age: 31

  • Voice Actor(s): Thomas Ridgewell, Ed Templer
  • Favourite Thing(s): His Guitar: Susan, Alcohol

  • Least Favourite Thing(s): Christmas
  • Hoodie Colour: Blue

Tord

Tord Is The Most Violent Character In The Show. He Leaves Pretty Early In The Series, But Comes Back In The Final Episode As A Bad Guy. In The Final Episode, He Comes Back To Get His Giant Robot.

  • Age: 29
  • Voice Actor(s): Tord Larsson, Jamie Spicer-Lewis
  • Favourite Thing(s): Guns, The Return Of The Insane Zombie Pirates IV, His Giant Robot
  • Least Favourite Thing(s): Tom, Edd, Sunshine Lollipops And Rainbows
  • Hoodie Colour: Red

Eduardo

You Can Tell Just From The Image That He Is Annoying. He Pretends That He Is Spanish, But The Only Thing He Can Say Is 'Soy Numero Uno' (Because He Is An Arrogant Piece Of Poo).

  • Age: 23
  • Voice Actor(s): Chris O' Neill, Brock Baker
  • Favourite Thing(s): Being Numero Uno
  • Least Favourite Thing(s): Edd, Edd, Edd
  • Jacket Colour: Green
